How It Works

This calculator shows your timeline to financial freedom by investing consistently in assets that generate passive income. The math is simple but powerful:

Monthly Passive Income = (Portfolio Value × Yearly Return) / 12

Your goal: once Monthly Passive Income ≥ Monthly Necessities, you have financial freedom.

Understanding the Variables

  • Your Age: Helps estimate your retirement timeline and adjust your strategy accordingly.
  • Monthly FFA Investment: How much you allocate to your Financial Freedom Account each month. This compounds over time.
  • Expected Yearly Return: Conservative (6%), Moderate (8–10%), Aggressive (12%+). Past market average is ~10%.
  • Monthly Necessities: Your target — once passive income matches this, you achieve financial freedom.

Real Examples

  • Conservative Path: RM 3,000/month × 8% return ≈ 20 years to RM 3,000 passive income
  • Moderate Path: RM 5,000/month × 10% return ≈ 16 years to RM 5,000 passive income
  • Aggressive Path: RM 10,000/month × 12% return ≈ 12 years to RM 7,500 passive income

Tips to Accelerate

  • Increase monthly investments to grow your FFA jar faster
  • Diversify investments to improve returns — stocks, real estate, bonds, business
  • Reduce unnecessary expenses to lower your necessities target
  • Reinvest passive income to compound growth faster
  • Develop additional income streams to increase FFA contributions

Important Notes

This calculator uses a simplified compound interest model. Real-world factors like inflation, market volatility, taxes, and fees are not included. The goal is to give you clarity on your financial freedom timeline and motivate consistent investing.
