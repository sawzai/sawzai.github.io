Frequently Asked Questions
What do the three challenges represent?
The Poverty Trap, The Comfort Keeper, and The Inner Blueprint are three core money patterns that consistently prevent people from building lasting wealth, regardless of income. Each challenge is designed to surface whether these patterns are active in your own financial decision-making.
How accurate is this as a financial assessment?
Each scenario is designed to reveal behavioural patterns that research consistently links to financial outcomes. It is not a diagnostic tool, but as a self-awareness instrument it is highly effective at surfacing the beliefs that drive financial results.
Why are the answer options shuffled each time?
The challenge randomises the order of answer options to ensure your responses reflect your genuine instincts rather than pattern recognition. Each scenario has three distinct approaches, any of which could appear in any position.
